What we are and what we do

Agrikart is a cutting edge startup that focuses on providing the best possible services to the farmers resided in remote locations of the country thereby helping them in the process of sowing fine quality seeds, maintenance services, tools required for farming and selling the final produce as well. Agri-Kart aims to build a complete solution for the nation builders so that they can achieve maximum yield of their produce.
